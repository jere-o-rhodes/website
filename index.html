Who Am I? 🤷

I'm a multimedia journalist currently based in the Bay Area.

Before I moved to California, I received my bachelor's degree in photo and video journalism from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

I'm currently a dean's merit fellow (still very surreal to say) at the University of California, Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism where I'm focusing on investigative and multimedia reporting.

Because I'm also very ✨professional ✨, I'm a member of the National Association of Black Journalists (NABJ) and the National Association of LGBTQ+ Journalists (NLGJA)

Where I've been

👀

After graduating from undergrad, I produced freelance video work for Univision and The Washington Post. I also worked on various independent film projects as a producer and cinematographer

While in graduate school, my work has been published in the San Francisco Chronicle, Oakland North and Berkelyside.

Where I've worked 💪

From 2021-2022, I was a video producer at The Tennessean , where I led various video projects and produced video strategies for newsrooms across the USA Today's South Network.

I'm currently a reporter with Fresnoland , a news organization that covers the San Joaquin Valley in California. There, I've focused primarily on housing coverage.

In the summer of 2023, I'll be working as an intern with Searchlight New Mexico , an investigative news organization based out of Santa Fe, NM.
